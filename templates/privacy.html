Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you interact with the app, such as pages visited and actions taken.

We do not collect payment or banking information of any kind.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the Spendly service.
  • Display your expense history and summaries within your account.
  • Improve the reliability and performance of the app.
  • Respond to support requests or account inquiries.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses a limited number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts — to load typefaces used in the interface. Google may log font requests; see Google's privacy policy for details.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics platforms, or social media trackers.

5. Cookies

Spendly uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in. This cookie is essential for the service to function and does not track you across other websites. No third-party cookies are set.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information provided on our website. We aim to respond to all enquiries within 5 business days.
