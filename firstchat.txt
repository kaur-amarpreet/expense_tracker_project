  project for students to build out step by step.

  What's already in place:
  - A landing page, login page, and registration page (rendered from HTML

  - Step 1 вҖ” Database setup (SQLite with get_db(), init_db(), seed_db())

  - Step 4 вҖ” User profile page
  - Step 7 вҖ” Add an expense
  - Step 8 вҖ” Edit an expense
  - Step 9 вҖ” Delete an expense

  It's essentially a teaching project where students progressively build a full
  CRUD expense tracking app with user authentication.
